  1. Chi is choice

    Laptop or Tablet? The 2-in-1 Chi gives you the best of both worlds. Take care of business with the powerful laptop and take it easy with the ultra slim tablet.

  2. Stylish mobility

    The ASUS Transformer Book T300 Chi is beautifully crafted with a streamline curve design, and a metallic cover with diamond cut edges. The result is a 7.6mm razor thin tablet and a sophisticated laptop measuring a slim 16.5mm.

  3. Pixel perfect

    Watching movies or just checking emails, it’s never looked better than on the Chi’s WQHD display. With a resolution of 2560 by 1440 pixels, and a class-leading pixel density of 235ppi, the ASUS Transformer Book Chi produces picture perfect images.

  4. Power up

    Get everything you want without the compromises. The ASUS Transformer Book T300 Chi has class-leading speed from its Intel® Core™ M processor and blazingly-fast data transfers from its all-flash storage. Whether it's for work or play, the ASUS Transformer Book T300 Chi has the power to get the job done.

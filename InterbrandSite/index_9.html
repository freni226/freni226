Brand Shop

On May 15th, 2015, ASUS opened its first flagship brand shop at the SYNTREND computer mall in Taipei. With the design concept of ‘Zensation’, the ASUS brand shop was designed by ASUS Design Center to create the most luxurious shopping experience for customers.

Corporate Events

CES 2015

ASUS Chairman Jonney Shih unveiled the company’s latest innovations during the Experience2morrow Press Event at the 2015 International Consumer Electronic Show (CES) in Las Vegas. Mr. Shih introduced the full lineup of the groundbreaking ASUS Transformer Book Chi Series — powerful, razor-thin 2-in-1 laptops that transform instantly into the world’s slimmest Windows tablets. Mr. Shih also unveiled the much-anticipated ZenFone 2, which features a refined ergonomic body, advanced PixelMaster camera technology, the latest Intel® Atom™ Z3580 processor, and the brand new version of ASUS ZenUI that is designed for ultimate personalization.

COMPUTEX 2015

ASUS presented its Zensation press event at Computex 2015, with Chairman Jonney Shih unveiling a stunning range of innovative products designed to bring harmony and simplicity to people’s personal and professional lives. The new products announced included ZenFone Selfie, a full-featured smartphone designed with advanced camera technology for capturing the best possible selfies; ZenPad 8.0, an 8-inch tablet with refined styling, incredible entertainment capabilities, and the world’s first tablet to offer customizable functionality; ZenPad S 8.0, the world’s first tablet with 4GB of RAM that delivers extreme performance with ultimate style; and Zen AiO Series, premium all-in-one PCs that provide a perfect balance of beauty and performance for an extraordinary home computing experience. Finally, for creative professionals, such as digital artists and photographers, Mr. Shih introduced ASUS ProArt PA329Q, a 32-inch 4K/UHD (ultra-high-definition) monitor that delivers breathtaking levels of detail.

iFA 2014

ASUS Corporate Vice President Eric Chen, accompanied by ASUS Design Center Vice President Mitch Yang announced the ASUS ZenWatch; ZenBook UX305, the world’s slimmest 13.3-inch QHD+ laptop; the compact and colorful EeeBook X205; and MeMO Pad 7 (ME572C), the latest and most stylish addition to the highly-successful MeMO Pad family of Android tablets.

Milan Design Week 2014

ASUS Design Center, the award-winning design center within ASUS, presented its Zensation exhibition at Milan Design Week 2015. Zensation showcased the design team's Zen-inspired philosophy of incorporating diverse elements to produce balanced, harmonious creations that provide incredible experiences and accessible beauty to users. The exhibition featured a creative installation by ASUS Design Center that combined water and sound to produce amazing effects for visitors, as well as a stunning product-experience area that had an immersive, animated backdrop and a wide range of ASUS products on display.

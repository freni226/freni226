About ASUS

ASUS is a worldwide top-three consumer notebook vendor and maker of the world’s best-selling, most award-winning motherboards. A leading enterprise in the new digital era, ASUS designs and manufactures products that perfectly meet the needs of today’s digital home and office, with a broad portfolio that includes motherboards, graphics cards, optical drives, displays, desktop and all-in-one PCs, notebooks, netbooks, servers, multimedia devices, wireless solutions, networking devices, tablets, smartphones and wearables. Driven by innovation and committed to quality, ASUS won 4,326 awards in 2014 and is widely credited with revolutionizing the PC industry with its Eee PC™. ASUS has more than 13,600 employees around the globe with a world-class R&D team of 4,500 engineers. Company revenue for 2014 was approximately US$14.5 billion.

Chairman’s Note

Global information and communications technology industries continue to experience rapid changes. Over the past year, ASUS has led the market with innovative design and quality. We started off as a humble motherboard manufacturer to become a global leading technology brand that provides users with almost every conceivable category of information technology, from notebooks, tablets, and peripherals to PC components and wearables. 2014 marked a significant year for ASUS, when we introduced our first stand alone smartphone, the ASUS ZenFone. It won the prestigious 2014 Red Dot Award for Product Design and also set new sales records as the ZenFone 5 model became Taiwan's best-selling smartphone. The successful launch of ZenFone opened up vast opportunities across global smartphone markets. We further showcased our innovation with the introduction of ZenWatch — the first ASUS wearable — along with Transformer Book Chi, ZenFone 2, and ZenFone Zoom at CES 2015. Continued development of smartphones, wearables and mobile products will be an ongoing focus for us. As technology advances and expands into more areas of everyday life, we will further our presence in cloud computing, mobile devices, wearables, home automation, robotics, and the Internet of Things (IoT). With our corporate brand promise and philosophy of In Search of Incredible in mind, ASUS will strive to create seamless and joyful digital experiences for everyone.

In Search of Incredible

ASUS is passionate about technology and driven by innovation. We dream, we dare and we strive to create an effortless and joyful digital life for everyone. We’re always in search of incredible ideas and experiences — and we aspire to deliver the incredible in everything we do.

2014 Revenue Mix

ASUSTek Computer Inc. ended 2014 on a high, with consolidated revenue hitting US$14.5 billion. Notebook and motherboards continue to achieve strong sales growth in various markets. Smartphones and tablets aim to grow substantially in 2015.

Leading Products

ASUS notebooks continue to achieve strong sales growth in various markets. We attribute this success to innovative design and quality. Our motherboards business won a global market-leader position many years in a row, and we continued to invest additional resources in an effort to cement our dominance in the sector. The successful launch of the ASUS ZenFone opened up vast new opportunities across global smartphone markets. The success of ZenFone was driven by a number of factors, such as excellent product design, new pricing strategy, efficient channel management, and extensive word-of-mouth marketing. In 2015 the Company will continue to innovate and differentiate devices in our popular notebook product lines, including ASUS Transformer Book Chi, EeeBook and Chromebook. In our smartphone product lines, ZenFone 2 and subsequent models will deepen the product concept of empowering luxury for everyone, allowing all consumers to enjoy the experience of high-quality products and services at affordable prices.

Market Trends 2014 – 2015

Innovation and quality are the driving forces behind the steady success of ASUS. The successful ZenFone Series and ZenWatch launches showed our dedication in entering the smartphone and Internet of Things (IoT) markets. ASUS also joined Show Chwan Health Care System and Chunghwa Telecom Co. to jointly promote the ASUS Cloud Platform for Health in Palau, which provides local residents with cloud services for use in healthcare. The ASUS brand spirit, In Search Of Incredible, is characterized by our relentless pursuit of quality and value for consumers who seek reliability, functionality and beauty. To this end, ASUS employs Design Thinking and the highest standards to push the boundaries of innovation and technology.

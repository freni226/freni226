Freedom, connection, and expression form the foundation for ASUS ZenUI, our sublime mobile user interface. The brand-new ZenUI has an exquisite visual design plus a wide range of new features — including ZenMotion, SnapView, Trend Micro™ Security and ZenUI Instant Updates — that enhance usability, ensure security and privacy, and deliver a seamless user experience.

ASUS ZenUI has more than 1,000 features that improve upon the standard Android interface.

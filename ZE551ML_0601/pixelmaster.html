Unrivaled Low Light Mode

Through a combination of pixel-merging technology, which joins four adjacent pixels to form a new pixel, and advanced image-processing algorithms, Low Light mode increases light sensitivity by up to 400%. It also enhances noise reduction and boosts color contrast by up to 400%, for clear and bright low-light shots with no need for flash.

Capture 4X More Details with Super Resolution

ZenFone 2 Super Resolution Mode uses special image-processing techniques (Multiframe Fusion Technology) to create incredibly-detailed photos. ZenFone 2 captures and combines four 13MP photos to create a single image of with exceptional 4X of clarity. Light or dark, you’ll enjoy more detail and less noise — a super resolution equivalent up to 52MP!
